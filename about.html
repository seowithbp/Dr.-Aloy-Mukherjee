About Dr. Aloy Mukherjee

Dr. Aloy Mukherjee is an internationally renowned Senior Consultant Surgeon with an exceptional track record of over 25 years. Specializing in Advanced Minimal Access Surgery, he is a pioneer in Laparoscopic, Bariatric, and Gastrointestinal procedures in India.

Currently practicing at the prestigious 2nd floor, Indraprastha Apollo Hospital, Room No. 1265, Gate No. 10, Jasola Vihar, New Delhi, Delhi 110076, Dr. Mukherjee focuses on providing patient-centered, ethical, and world-class surgical care utilizing the latest technological advancements including Robotic Surgery.

Our Mission

To provide exceptional surgical and bariatric care that restores mobility, alleviates pain, and enhances the quality of life for every patient through innovative minimally-invasive treatments and compassionate service.

We are committed to making world-class surgical treatment accessible and affordable for all patients, ensuring that everyone receives the care they deserve.

Our Vision

To be the leading surgical center in India, recognized for clinical excellence, innovative laparoscopic treatments, and outstanding patient outcomes.

We aspire to set new standards in surgical care through continuous research, advanced robotic technology adoption, and a patient-centric approach to healing.
